What is Sales Training?

Most salespeople hit a plateau at some point in their careers even if they’ve attended general corporate training
courses. Sales training courses help sales teams to upgrade their techniques, empathise with their sales prospects
and close more deals for their companies to improve the bottom-line.

Why Sales Training?

According to Forbes magazine, 55% of salespeople lack basic sales skills. Most salespeople tend to forget about
serving their customer’s needs first before filing their bottom-lines. Learning to handle rejection, identifying
and qualifying customer profiles and preparing for sales presentations and mastery of negotiation techniques
are important skills modern salespeople need to possess. We’ve found that even after attending effective
presentation skills courses, certain salespeople still lack the negotiation nuances to excel at work.
